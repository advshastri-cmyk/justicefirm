Common Legal Questions

Brief answers to frequently asked questions under Indian law.

How long does it take to get bail?

Bail timelines depend on the nature of offence, court jurisdiction and urgency. Some matters may be heard on the same day.

What is anticipatory bail?

Anticipatory bail is protection from arrest granted before arrest, subject to court conditions and discretion.

How long does a divorce case take?

Mutual consent divorces may conclude quicker, while contested matters depend on evidence and court workload.

Can an FIR be quashed?

FIRs may be quashed by the High Court in appropriate cases to prevent abuse of legal process.
